Nissan Datsun DIGIT Project

This project will show the evolution of the Datsun model from Nissan.

Nissan Motors originated in 1933. Yoshisuke Aikawa become the president in 1928. He debuted on the Tokyo Stock Exchange by it's ticker name NISSAN. Nissan then expanded beyond it's original Japanese roots, expanding into the U.S. as Datsun in 1958.
