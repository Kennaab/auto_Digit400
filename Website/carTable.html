Table of Years with Cars Manufactured

One interesting fact that is observable from this timeline; there is a major halt in new Datsun models from the years 1938 to 1948. What would cause such a thing? Well, starting from the year 1939, a small thing called World War II started and it lasted all the way until 1945. Nissan stopped its Datsun productions then as it focused on military trucks and aircraft.
